Paws for a Cause — Adoption Fair

March 1, 2025 • 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Central Park Community Center

Join us for our biggest adoption event of the year! We'll have dozens of cats, dogs, and other animals looking for their forever homes.

The event will feature:

• Free veterinary consultations
• Pet care demonstrations
• Children's activities
• Food trucks and refreshments
• Adoption application assistance

Admission is free, but we encourage donations to support our shelter operations. Come early for the best selection of adorable companions!

Pet Health Awareness Workshop

March 15, 2025 • 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Animal Wellness Center

Our team of veterinarians will share essential information about keeping your pets healthy and happy.

Workshop topics include:

• Preventative health care
• Nutrition and diet
• Exercise requirements
• Common health issues
• When to seek veterinary care

Space is limited to ensure quality interaction with our veterinary staff. Please register in advance to secure your spot.
