Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our adoption process

How can we help you?

Adoption Process

How does the adoption process work?

Our adoption process typically follows these steps:

  1. Browse available pets on our website or visit our shelter
  2. Complete an adoption application
  3. Meet with an adoption counselor for an interview
  4. Meet your potential pet (we encourage multiple visits)
  5. Home visit (for certain animals)
  6. Approval and adoption fee payment
  7. Take your new family member home!

The entire process usually takes 1-7 days, depending on the specific circumstances and animal.

Can I place a hold on an animal I'm interested in?

Yes, we offer a 24-hour hold on animals for potential adopters. A small deposit (₱500) is required, which will be applied to your adoption fee if you proceed with adoption. If you decide not to adopt, the deposit becomes a donation to help our shelter animals.

Please note that holds are available on a first-come, first-served basis, and we can only accommodate holds for people who have completed an application and initial screening.

How long does the adoption process take?

From application to taking your pet home, the process typically takes 1-7 days. Some adoptions can be completed same-day if all requirements are met and the match is clearly appropriate. More complex situations—such as first-time pet owners, certain breeds with special needs, or homes with multiple existing pets—may require additional time for counseling and preparation.

Our goal is to ensure successful, lasting matches rather than rushing the process.

Requirements

What are the requirements to adopt a pet?

Our basic adoption requirements include:

  • Must be at least 21 years of age
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Proof of residence (utility bill, lease agreement, etc.)
  • If renting, landlord verification of pet policy
  • All household members must meet the pet before adoption
  • Current pets must be up-to-date on vaccinations

Additional requirements may apply for specific animals or breeds.

Can I adopt if I live in an apartment?

Absolutely! Many of our animals thrive in apartment settings. However, we do consider the animal's specific needs, energy level, and size when determining if they're a good match for apartment living.

If you rent, you'll need to provide proof that pets are allowed in your building and any pet deposits have been arranged. We may also consider factors like access to outdoor spaces, exercise opportunities, and your lifestyle to ensure a suitable match.

Do you allow out-of-state adoptions?

We generally focus on local adoptions to ensure we can conduct proper home visits and follow-ups. However, we do consider out-of-state adoptions on a case-by-case basis, typically within a 100-mile radius of our shelter.

For out-of-state adoptions, we may partner with rescue organizations in your area to conduct home visits and may require additional references. Transportation of the animal would be your responsibility, and additional paperwork may be needed for interstate animal transport.

Fees & Costs

What are your adoption fees?

Our adoption fees vary by animal type, age, and special needs:

  • Dogs: ₱500-₱1000 (puppies typically higher)
  • Cats: ₱500-₱1000 (kittens typically higher)
  • Small Animals: ₱500-₱750

These fees help cover spay/neuter surgery, microchipping, vaccinations, deworming, and basic medical care while in our shelter. Senior animals and those with special needs often have reduced fees.

We occasionally run special promotions with reduced adoption fees for certain animals.

What is included in the adoption fee?

Our adoption fees include:

  • Spay/neuter surgery
  • Microchip and registration
  • Age-appropriate vaccinations
  • Deworming treatment
  • Flea/tick treatment
  • Basic veterinary exam
  • 30 days of pet health insurance
  • Starter bag of food
  • Post-adoption support and resources

The actual value of these services if purchased separately would be significantly higher than our adoption fees.

Do you offer any discounts on adoption fees?

Yes, we offer several discount programs:

  • Senior Pets (7+ years): 50% off regular fees
  • Senior Citizens (65+): 25% off adoption fees
  • Military/Veterans: 25% off adoption fees
  • "Buddy System" (adopting two bonded animals): 25% off total fee
  • Long-term Residents (4+ months in shelter): Reduced fees

We also run seasonal promotions throughout the year. Follow our social media for announcements!

Pet Care

What support do you offer after adoption?

We're committed to supporting you throughout your pet's life. Our post-adoption services include:

  • 30-day follow-up check-in
  • Behavior hotline for questions and concerns
  • Training resources and recommendations
  • Monthly new adopter support group meetings
  • Educational workshops and webinars
  • Referrals to pet-friendly resources

If you encounter challenges with your adopted pet, please contact us before considering surrender. We can often provide resources to help resolve issues.

What should I prepare before bringing my new pet home?

For Dogs:

  • Appropriately sized crate/bed
  • Food and water bowls
  • High-quality dog food (we'll tell you what they've been eating)
  • Collar, ID tag, and leash
  • Toys for mental stimulation
  • Pet-safe cleaning supplies
  • Baby gates if needed for space management

For Cats:

  • Litter box and litter
  • Food and water dishes
  • High-quality cat food
  • Scratching post
  • Toys and enrichment items
  • Carrier for transport
  • Comfortable bed or resting space

We provide a detailed "Going Home" guide specific to your new pet during the adoption process.

What if the adoption doesn't work out?

While we strive to make perfect matches, we understand that sometimes unforeseen circumstances arise. Our return policy includes:

  • 14-day trial period with full refund of adoption fee
  • Lifetime return policy (we always accept our animals back)
  • Behavior consultation before returns to try addressing issues
  • Partial refund of adoption fee (on case-by-case basis) for returns after 14 days but within 30 days

Please never rehome an adopted pet on your own. Contact us first, as we want to ensure the animal's continued wellbeing and have valuable information about their history and needs.
