Welcome to TAILS -
The Animal Initiative for Love & Support

At TAILS, we believe that every animal deserves love, safety, and a chance to live a happy life. Our mission is to rescue abandoned, neglected, and abused animals, provide them with compassionate care, and connect them with individuals and families who can offer a forever home. From urgent rescue efforts to community outreach and volunteer programs, we are committed to making a difference—one paw at a time. Whether you're here to adopt, volunteer, donate, or simply learn more, you're joining a community that stands for kindness, hope, and second chances. Because at TAILS, love isn't just a word—it's our promise to every animal we meet.

If you’re ready to make a difference, we’d love to hear from you. Reach out through our contact page or explore more about what we do. Together, we can give every animal the future they deserve.
