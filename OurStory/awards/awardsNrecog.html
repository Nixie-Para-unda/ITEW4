OUR JOURNEY

How We Raised 1M to Support Pet Adoption

At TAILS, our journey began with a deep passion for animal welfare and the belief that every pet deserves a loving home. Over the years, we've worked tirelessly to raise 34 million pesos, funding vital programs and initiatives that make a real difference for pets across the Philippines.

Through collaboration with local shelters, volunteers, and generous donors, we have been able to expand our reach and provide life-saving services to countless animals in need. From rescue operations to adoption campaigns, each milestone in our journey is a testament to the incredible community that has rallied around us.

Thank you for being part of our journey. Together, we continue to make a meaningful impact in the lives of both pets and families.

About Us
