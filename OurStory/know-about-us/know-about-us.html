Know About Us

We are TAILS – a passionate nonprofit team working globally to create loving futures for animals in need

At TAILS (The Animal Initiative for Love and Support), our mission is to give every animal a second chance at life through compassionate rescue, rehabilitation, and adoption. We believe that every tail deserves a happy ending — and we work tirelessly to make that happen.

Founded on the belief that love knows no species, our dedicated team operates across borders, partnering with local shelters, volunteers, and vet clinics to ensure that abandoned and mistreated animals find safe, forever homes. From street rescues to community outreach, TAILS is committed to creating a world where no animal is left behind. Every adoption isn't just a happy story — it's a powerful transformation. Our work goes beyond rescue; we focus on education, responsible pet ownership, and lifelong support for adopters to ensure each match is a lasting one.

Our Mission

We make the world a better place for animals in need

We are committed to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in distress. Through our network of volunteers, veterinarians, and community partners, we provide immediate care and long-term solutions for animals facing abandonment, abuse, or neglect. Our mission extends beyond rescue to education and advocacy, as we work to create a world where all animals are treated with compassion and respect.

Our Vision

Creating a world where every animal has a loving home

We envision a future where animal shelters are no longer necessary because every pet has a home. Through community education, accessible veterinary care, and compassionate adoption programs, we strive to build a world where animals are valued as family members, not possessions. Our vision includes sustainable rescue operations, innovative adoption solutions, and a global network of animal welfare advocates working together.
